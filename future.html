Future Goals

I have my academic goals, but I also have general goals for the future. Once I get my career in order, I want to leave the city. I've lived in Los Angeles my whole life, and I've gotten tired of urban life. In the future, I'd like to go back and visit my birthplace in North Carolina. Maybe in the far future I'll live in Alaska. It seems like a quiet place I might like. I'd also like to travel, but my biggest goal is to get to a point where I can be at peace. I'm tired of being unsure and stressed about everything.
